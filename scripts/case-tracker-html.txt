        <h2 style="border-bottom:none;">Privacy Policy</h2>
        <p style="color:var(--muted); font-size:0.875rem;">Last updated: April 2026</p>

        <h3>1. Purpose</h3>
        <p>The "Israeli Legal Case Finder" extension scans web pages the user visits, identifies references to Israeli court case numbers (by known numbering patterns), and retrieves case summaries from an external legal database. Its sole purpose is to help lawyers, researchers, and citizens understand the legal context in the page they are reading.</p>

        <h3>2. Data Collected</h3>
        <p>When the extension identifies legal case references on a page, it sends the following to the server:</p>
        <ul>
          <li><strong>Case numbers</strong> identified (e.g., HCJ 1234/05)</li>
          <li>A <strong>short text snippet</strong> (~200 characters) around each reference, for relevance ranking only</li>
          <li>The <strong>page URL</strong> where references were found</li>
          <li>The <strong>page title</strong></li>
        </ul>
        <p>The extension does <strong>not</strong> read or transmit: forms, passwords, cookies, emails, messages, general browsing history, or personally identifying information (except Google account for authentication).</p>

        <h3>3. Where Data Is Sent</h3>
        <p>Data is sent to the <strong>Tag-It</strong> (Smart-DMS) service at <code style="background:#f3f4f6; padding:2px 6px; border-radius:4px; font-size:0.9em;">tag-it.biz</code>, an Israeli legal database. Some summaries are generated using large language models (AI/LLM) on the Tag-It server side. The extension itself does not send data directly to AI providers.</p>

        <h3>4. Data Usage</h3>
        <p>Data is used solely for the extension's single purpose. Data is <strong>not</strong> used for advertising, marketing, profiling, creditworthiness, lending, or any unrelated purpose. Data is not sold or transferred to third parties beyond the Tag-It service.</p>

        <h3>5. Authentication</h3>
        <p>The extension supports Google sign-in for authentication with Tag-It. Login credentials are stored locally in the browser only.</p>

        <h3>6. Retention &amp; Deletion</h3>
        <p>Retrieved summaries are cached locally for 24 hours. Users can clear the cache at any time. Server-side data is subject to Tag-It's retention policy.</p>

        <h3>7. Chrome Web Store Limited Use Compliance</h3>
        <div class="warning-box">
          <ul>
            <li>Use of user data is limited to providing and improving the extension's single purpose.</li>
            <li>User data is not transferred to third parties except as needed to provide the service, comply with the law, or ensure security.</li>
            <li>User data is not used for advertising or purposes unrelated to the extension's single purpose.</li>
            <li>Humans do not read user data except with explicit consent, for security, or for legal compliance.</li>
          </ul>
